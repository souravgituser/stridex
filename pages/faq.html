FAQ & Help Center

Got questions? We've got answers. Explore our comprehensive help categories below.

Shipping & Order Status

How can I track my StrideX order?

Once your order is processed and leaves our warehouse, we will email you a secure Tracking Link. You can also view the status dynamically directly on your Order Confirmation page by copying your unique Order ID.

What are the shipping rates and estimated delivery times?

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $150. For other shipping options and detailed timelines, please visit our dedicated Shipping & Delivery page.

Sizing & Fitting

Do StrideX shoes fit true to size (TTS)?

Yes, our performance running lines fit true to size. If you have particularly wide arches, we offer select Wide (2E) sizing options. For precise measurements, please review our comprehensive Size Guide.

Returns & Exchanges

What is the StrideX return policy?

We offer a premium 30-Day Hassle-Free return policy. Shoes must be in their original packaging and in unworn condition. For complete eligibility terms and instructions, visit our Returns & Exchanges portal.

Payments & Security

Which payment methods do you accept?

We support major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), PayPal, and secure bank transfers/UPI.
