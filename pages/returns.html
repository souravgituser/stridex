Returns & Exchanges

Not the perfect fit? No problem. We provide a premium 30-Day Hassle-Free return or exchange period for all footwear orders.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Footwear must be in unworn, clean condition with all original packaging (the shoe box must not be defaced).
  • Custom orders and final clearance sales are not eligible for refunds or exchanges.

How to Start Your Return

1

Initiate Request

Click Contact Support or email returns@stridex.com with your Order ID.

2

Print Pre-paid Label

We will email you a pre-paid FedEx return shipping label within 24 hours.

3

Drop Off & Refund

Drop the package at any FedEx station. Refunds clear in 3 - 5 business days.
