Technology Inside StrideX

Interact with the active dots on the shoe to reveal core architectural specs.

1. RipShield Knitted Upper

Densely woven engineered nylon fiber wraps the arch, providing dynamic lateral stabilization, custom lock-fit comfort, and water-repelling shell shield.

2. Nitrogen-Infused Active Foam

By introducing pressurized nitrogen molecules into HSL polymers during foam casting, we created a cushion structure that weighs 35% less and offers 80% higher bounce.

3. Curved Carbon Propulsion Shaft

A dual-curved responsive carbon composite plate runs from midfoot to toe. Actively bends on foot strike, releasing snapping horizontal force during push-off.
